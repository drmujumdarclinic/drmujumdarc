About Dr. Mujumdar Clinic

Established in 2001, Dr. Mujumdar Clinic in Ahilyanagar is known for its trusted care and modern medical practices. Our mission is to provide affordable, efficient, and compassionate healthcare to families in our community.

We specialize in family medicine, child care, general checkups, emergency care, and lab testing. Our experienced medical team is always ready to serve you with empathy and expertise.
